## 1. WHOIS & DNS
*Technique: Identifying ownership and mapping the IP space.*

### WHOIS
* **Command:** `whois target.com`
* **Look for:** Admin email, Registrar, Creation Date.

### DNS Interrogation
* **Tools:** `dig`, `nslookup`, `dnsrecon`
* **Zone Transfer (The "Holy Grail"):**
    * Try to copy all DNS records (rarely works now but must check).
    * Command: `dig axfr @ns1.target.com target.com`

## 2. Network & Email
*Technique: Mapping the path to the target.*

### Network Topology
* **Traceroute:** identifying hops and firewalls.
    * Linux: `traceroute target.com`
    * Windows: `tracert target.com`

### Email Headers
* **Technique:** Analyze "Received" headers to find internal IPs.
* **Tool:** [MXToolbox](https://mxtoolbox.com/)
